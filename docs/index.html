How it works

Up and running in four steps.

01

Download

Grab the latest release for macOS, Windows, or Linux from GitHub.

02

Add your key

Open Settings → AI Providers and enter your OpenAI API key.

03

Record

Hit start when the meeting begins. Stop when it ends.

04

Review

Open the session and read the Summary and Transcript tabs.

Local-first by design

Your sessions stay on your machine. Recordings and transcripts are stored locally — not on a cloud server. You bring your own OpenAI key for transcription and summaries; session data stays under your control. Stored in meetify.db in your app data folder.

macOS may block the download — how do I open Meetify?

Mac builds are not yet code-signed or notarized. After downloading from GitHub Releases, macOS may show “Meetify is damaged and can’t be opened.” The app isn’t broken — Gatekeeper is rejecting an unsigned download.

After moving Meetify to Applications, run:

xattr -cr /Applications/Meetify.app

Then open Meetify normally. Alternatively, right-click the app → Open the first time.

Signed and notarized Mac releases are planned so this step won’t be needed.
